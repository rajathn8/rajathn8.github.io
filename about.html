My Story

I started my journey in tech in 2017, fresh out of Visvesvaraya Technological University with an engineering degree. My first role at IBM introduced me to data analytics and automation - and I was hooked.

Over the years, I've worked with incredible teams. At NextOrbit, I built data pipelines processing gigabytes in real-time. At Infoblox, I dove into network security. The defining chapter came at Goldman Sachs, where I spent three years as Vice President building high-performance systems on AWS.

Today, I'm at Intropic, working at the intersection of AI and financial analytics. I'm passionate about leveraging GenAI to build smarter, more intuitive systems.

Adventures
